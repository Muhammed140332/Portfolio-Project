About Me

I am a Product Designer

From Lagos. I enjoy creating meaningful and delightful user-centred digital products that balance user needs and meet business goals. Currently freelancing in Lagos.

I've had the opportunity to solve problems in both startup and enterprise environments on projects related to ecommerce, healthcare, social life, fintech solution, and real estate. I have worked with a host of startups solving complex design problems around human needs.

With a background in both agriculture and design, I love connecting the dots between trading, business goals and user satisfaction. In my free time, I enjoy poetry and looking for last minute adventures.
